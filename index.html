What is Denk Development?

Denk Development is a German hardware and software development company, operated by me, Timo Denk. It was founded because I got lots of requests from people on the internet who saw my projects, especially on YouTube, and wanted me to develop stuff for them. In order to do this Denk Development was founded in June 2016.
